Architecture

Above is the complete architecture for the Membership Registration App. By leveraging the Salesforce App Cloud, we can now offer customers an amazing and seamless membership registration experience.

Additionally, because we are using Heroku Connect to synchronize data between Heroku and Salesforce, our internal business users can manage all membership tiers, new registrations, and members in their primary business system, Salesforce

  • Salesforce

    Salesforce is the leading cloud platform as a service and CRM on the planet!

    For this example app, Salesforce is our business system to manage members, membership options, and allows for membership management

  • Heroku Connect

    Using bi-directional synchronization between Salesforce and Heroku Postgres, Heroku Connect unifies the data in your Postgres database with members, membership contacts, memberships, and membership tiers

    NO coding or complex configuration is required

  • Heroku Postgres - HEROKU ADDON

    The SQL database service for developers.

    Easy to Set Up, Secure by Default

  • Auth0 - HEROKU ADDON

    Out of the box authentication and authorization for customer facing apps

    Don't spend time reinventing the wheel - get the app launched and provide a SECURE authenticated experience!
